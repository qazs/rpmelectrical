about us

Setup in the year 2000, RPM Electrical Engineering Pte Ltd has established itself as one of the stalwarts of electrical and mechanical engineering solutions in Singapore. We have built our reputation on reliable, committed and strong customer support. We provide equipment testing services, machining and fabrication, as well as being a stockist of motor accessories. As a forward-thinking company, we are ever catering to the needs of our partners, expanding our services to meet their needs.

In meeting our partners’ requirements, RPM Electrical Engineering customises our colutions for them. we present a unique, one-stop service provider to our partners, ensuring their needs and wants are met.

RPM Electrical Engineering Pte Ltd is located at No. 1 Joo Koon Way, Singapore. Where our experience staff and skilled workers provide prompt and friendly services at all times.
